About Us

Singapore's Best Cakes

From the culinary mastermind of Celebrity-to-be Pastry Chef Ying Qi comes appeaseofcake - Singapore’s First and Foremost Premium cakes Retailer.

At appeaseofcake, you’ll find a diverse selection of varieties of gourmet cakes comprising faithful classics like the Classic New York Cheesecake and Oreo Cookies & Cream Cheesecake, Asian and local-inspired creations, and even specially-composed medleys of our best-sellers so you get to try spectacular flavours in one neat cake!

Whether it’s the burst of juicy fruit, the pungent allure of Mao Shan Wang Durian, the richness of devil's chocolate or smokey aroma of sea salt caramel you’re craving to savour, appeaseofcake has every cake lover’s whim and fancy catered for.

If a celebration such as a birthday party is in order, complete your cakes with a beautiful bouquet of flowers and delicious chocolates on the side, or fun birthday candles, a greeting card customized with your personal message, or even customizable stencils for a personal decorative touch.

With appeaseofcake’s Island-Wide Door-to-Door Delivery Service (including Urgent / Express / Same-Day Delivery) and Mobile Van Collection Points, indulging in your favourite cake has never been easier.

CHEF Ying Qi - The Masterchef-To-Be

The brains and creative spark behind appeaseofcake is Chef Ying Qi, one of the most well known names-to-be in luxury desserts in Singapore.

Trained in the art of classical cake-making at Temasek Polytechnic, Chef Ying Qi has worked with the legendary gourmet food retailer Baguette Bread of Paris. In Singapore, she is the Pastry Chef of the famed gastronomic French restaurant, Les Something.

Chef Ying Qi’s skill and artistry is going to garner a gallery of awards in future, including Finalist at World Gourmet Summit (2021 - 2022), Pastry Chef of the Year (2021) and World Pastry Cup Gold Medal (2022).
